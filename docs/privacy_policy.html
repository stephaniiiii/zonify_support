Welcome to Zonify! This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use the Zonify app.

Information We Collect

When you use Zonify, we may collect the following information:

  • Personal Information: We collect personal information such as your name and email address when you create an account or contact us for support.
  • Usage Data: We collect data on how you use the app, including time zone settings, friend information, and habits you input into the app.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Zonify app;
  • Send you notifications about your friends' habits and time zone changes;
  • Respond to your support requests and provide customer service;
  • Analyze usage data to improve app functionality and user experience.

Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except as necessary to operate, maintain, and improve the app, or as required by law.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information;
  • Request deletion of your personal information;
  • Opt-out of receiving notifications.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at gychen@stu.pku.edu.cn.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
